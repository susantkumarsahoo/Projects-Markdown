## How to use

- **Replace images:** Put your PNGs/JPGs in the `images/` folder using the same filenames, or update the links.
- **Keep captions short:** One line explaining what the chart communicates.
- **Optimize size:** Export at ~2× scale with transparent or white background.
- **License note:** If you use sample data, include a brief note about its source.
